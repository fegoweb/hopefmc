MISSION STATEMENT

At Hope Family Medical Center we believe that although we cannot always cure we can always comfort. Hope Family Medical Center is founded on patient centered, caring principles to provide affordable, quality medical services, health education, and health and wellness related resources to all those in need. At Hope Family Medical Center we believe that everyone should be able to receive quality health services from caring providers in a holistic environment of caring and comfort.



THOSE WITH INSURANCE

Patients who have medical insurance with an “out of network” benefit and/or who have a flex spending account can also save on services received at our clinic. For those with an “out of network” benefit we will give you a comprehensive encoded billing statement to submit to your health insurance company for reimbursement. And, if you have a flex spending account you can use your card to pay for all your medical needs at our clinic. Please speak to our billing specialist about how Hope FMC can help you and your family. THOSE WITH NO INSURANCE

We provide individuals and families who are without health insurance lower-cost medical care for all primary and urgent medical care needs. We also offer a significant savings on the majority of labs, x-rays, and medical procedures. Please speak to our billing specialist about how Hope Family Medical Center can help you and your family.



THOSE WITH HIGH DEDUCTIBLES

We also provide individuals and families who have high deductibles lower-cost medical care. Use your insurance only for your major medical costs and visit HOPE Family Medical for all your primary and urgent care needs. The cost of your visits can be applied to your annual deductible. Please speak to our billing specialist about how Hope Family Medical Center can help you and your family.
